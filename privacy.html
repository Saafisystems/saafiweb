1. Overview

Saafi Systems ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with us.

This policy applies to all Saafi Systems services, including our SaaS platforms, PaaS solutions, education management systems, ERP software, and EMR applications. By using our services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

Important: This policy is compliant with GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA, and other international privacy regulations. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of data protection.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Register for an account or subscribe to our services
  • Request information about our products or services
  • Participate in surveys, contests, or promotional activities
  • Contact us for customer support
  • Apply for employment opportunities

This information may include:

  • Identity Data: Name, username, title, date of birth
  • Contact Data: Email address, telephone numbers, billing and delivery addresses
  • Financial Data: Bank account and payment card details
  • Transaction Data: Details about payments and services you have purchased
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, time zone setting, operating system
  • Profile Data: Username, password, purchases, preferences, feedback
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website and services
  • Marketing Data: Your preferences in receiving marketing communications

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you access our services, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Log files and server data
  • Device information (device type, operating system, browser type)
  • Usage patterns and preferences
  • Location data (with your consent)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2.3 Special Categories of Data

For our healthcare solutions (EMR systems), we may process special categories of personal data, including health information, but only:

  • With your explicit consent
  • When necessary for healthcare purposes
  • In compliance with applicable healthcare regulations (HIPAA, etc.)
  • Under strict security and confidentiality measures

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

3.1 Service Provision

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our services
  • Process transactions and manage your account
  • Deliver customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Send service-related communications

3.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our services
  • Develop new features and functionality
  • Conduct research and analytics
  • Perform quality assurance and testing

3.3 Communication

  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Provide updates about our services
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Send administrative information

3.4 Legal and Security

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect against fraud and security threats
  • Enforce our terms of service
  • Resolve disputes and troubleshoot problems

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on: (1) Your consent, (2) Performance of a contract, (3) Compliance with legal obligations, (4) Protection of vital interests, (5) Performance of tasks in the public interest, or (6) Legitimate interests pursued by us or third parties.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as described in this policy. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in:

  • Cloud hosting and data storage
  • Payment processing
  • Email delivery and communication services
  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Customer support services
  • Security and fraud prevention

These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the specified purposes.

4.2 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or part of our business, your personal information may be transferred to the acquiring entity, subject to the same privacy protections.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or in response to:

  • Court orders or legal process
  • Government investigations
  • Regulatory compliance requirements
  • Protection of our rights and property
  • Prevention of fraud or illegal activities

4.4 Consent-Based Sharing

We may share your information with your explicit consent for specific purposes, such as:

  • Integration with third-party applications
  • Participation in joint marketing campaigns
  • Research and development partnerships

5. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our security practices include:

5.1 Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3 and at rest using AES-256
  • Access Controls: Multi-factor authentication and role-based access controls
  • Network Security: Firewalls, intrusion detection systems, and regular security monitoring
  • Secure Infrastructure: SOC 2 Type II certified data centers with 24/7 monitoring
  • Regular Updates: Automated security patches and vulnerability assessments

5.2 Administrative Safeguards

  • Employee background checks and security training
  • Confidentiality agreements and access restrictions
  • Incident response procedures and breach notification protocols
  • Regular security audits and compliance assessments

5.3 Physical Safeguards

  • Secure data center facilities with biometric access controls
  • Environmental controls and redundant power systems
  • Secure disposal of hardware and storage media

Security Notice: While we implement industry-leading security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We continuously monitor and improve our security practices.

When personal information is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it using industry-standard methods. You may also request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain legal and contractual obligations.

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

For our education management systems, we may process student information under the supervision of educational institutions, in compliance with FERPA and other applicable regulations.
